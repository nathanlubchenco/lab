COMMANDER

you direct the toons — you don't fly them

click a soldier to select  ·  click ground/hostile to order them (or everyone)  ·  Esc deselect  ·  123 mode  ·  N arena
Zero-G, book rules: soldiers change course only by pushing off walls, star blocks, and bodies — waypoints snap to surfaces, mid-drift is committed, collisions shove, and you can kick off a frozen enemy. You are always outnumbered; the enemy commander draws a random doctrine each battle (watch the OPFOR readout), guards hold their gate, hunters stalk your wounded. WEGO: time freezes while you queue chained orders (limited comms per turn); Space runs 2.5 s of battle. DIRECT: real-time, instant orders. CLASSIC: real-time with comms lag. Win every arena: freeze them all, or hold their gate.
