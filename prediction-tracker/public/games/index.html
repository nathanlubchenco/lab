ENDER GAMES

Three single-player prototypes inspired by Ender's Game. Which is most fun?

A · holo arcade

Light Duel

The Ch.5 game-room duel. Bait an over-aggressive AI into committing, then juke during its lag window. Best 2 of 3.

 B · zero-G

Battle Room

Float through the cube on pure momentum. Freeze enemy soldiers or reach their gate. The enemy's gate is down.

 C · command

Commander

Direct a squad, not a soldier. Orders lag and your toons obey imperfectly. Think like Ender.
